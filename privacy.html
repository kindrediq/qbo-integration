Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2028

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Kindred IQ Solutions Inc. (“Provider”) collects, accesses, and uses data through the QuickBooks Online Integration (“Application”).

The Application is designed solely to integrate data from a Customer’s QuickBooks Online account for the Customer’s internal business purposes.

By authorizing or using the Application, the Customer agrees to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Accessed

With the Customer’s authorization, the Application may access data from the Customer’s QuickBooks Online account, which may include:

  • Accounting and financial records
  • Transaction data (such as invoices, payments, vendors, and customers)
  • Company and account metadata

The Application accesses this data only through OAuth authorization provided by Intuit.

3. Use of Data

Customer data is accessed and processed only for the purpose of:

  • Performing the Customer‑requested integration
  • Transferring or synchronizing data to Customer‑approved systems
  • Reporting, analytics, or processing for internal business use

Provider does not use Customer data for marketing, profiling, or any unrelated purpose.

4. Data Ownership

All data accessed through the Application remains the sole property of the Customer.

Provider does not claim ownership of, or rights to, Customer data.

5. Data Sharing

Customer data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Customer data may be processed or stored within Microsoft Azure infrastructure solely as necessary to operate the Application.

Data is disclosed only as required to provide the Application’s functionality or as required by applicable law.

6. Hosting and Security

The Application is hosted on Microsoft Azure infrastructure.

Provider implements reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect Customer data, including access controls and secure authentication mechanisms.

7. Data Retention

Customer data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the Application or as required by the Customer.

When access to the Application is revoked, stored credentials and access tokens are revoked or deleted, and the Application no longer accesses Customer data.

8. Revocation of Access

The Customer may revoke the Application’s access at any time by disconnecting the Application from QuickBooks Online.

Upon revocation:

  • OAuth tokens are invalidated or removed
  • The Application ceases all access to QuickBooks Online data

9. Changes to This Policy

Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Continued use of the Application after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
